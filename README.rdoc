= Blog King

A collection of widgets and plugins for wordpress > 2.8

Download the folders into your /wp-content/plugins/ folder. Activate in admin area and youre good to go.

The King Widgets are pretty advanced, since they are stuffed with options.

Details can be found here:

http://www.blog.mediaprojekte.de/wordpress-widgets-plugins/
